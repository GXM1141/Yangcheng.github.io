Brief Bio

I'm the Associate professor in School of Computer Sciences of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, the Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science and Technology of Tsinghua University in 2019. The main research interests include data mining, machine learning, nautual language processing and social computing. I have published nearly 50 refereed papers, including top journals and conferences in data mining and machine learning, such as IEEE TKDE, ACM TOIS, KDD, WWW, EMNLP, AAAI and IJCAI. I served as a member of the program committee and reviewer of journals at top domestic and foreign conferences including ACL, EMNLP, SMP, etc.
